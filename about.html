We. Love. Games.

Obscuratus Gaming develops games designed to bring people together.

Obscuratus Gaming (OG) is a fledgling gaming collaborative begun recently by four friends drawn together by an obsession with games. We've played games of all kinds, all of our lives: individual and team sports, board games,video games, mobile games, and most recently, games involving virtual reality. We believe games bring people of all ages together. They entertain and educate; encourage imagination, curiosity, and a sense of adventure; and they delight. Of course, they can also frustrate and annoy, and that’s why we’re here!
