OUR STORY

It was an epic yard sale. Amy had quite the game collection, stored safely in her attic. People loved giving her board and card games for birthdays and holidays. Her neighbors, Cheyenne, Kyle and Jason, brought their own seldom-used games to add to the sale. Amy joked, "We should just open a store!" So we did. A few years later we've added our own game creations to our store stock. Donations from friends and family forced us to find larger locations and warehouse storage.

From this unexpected beginning, our love for games and gaming has grown, our passion driving us to explore distant locations and learn from other cultures. Our annual trips and explorations have sparked developmentof ever more vivid and realistic game environments and storylines.

Our goal is to immerse you in new worlds, engage you with new gaming challenges, educate you about the “whole wide world” in its many miraculous variations, and entertain you by creating game environments rich in the environment, heritage, and traditions of new cultures.

Our stock is rising! Whether you're an investor, a collector, a gamer or someone looking to get rid of your dusty collection, we are looking forward to working with you.
