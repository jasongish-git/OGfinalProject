We. Love. Games.

Obscuratus Gaming is a collaborative gaming studio created by four friends who have always been obsessed with games.

We play, design, create, and sell games that bring people together.

Here at Obscuratus Gaming, we've played games of all kinds, all of our lives: individual and team sports, board games, video games, mobile games, and most recently, games involving virtual reality.

We've learned that games bring people together. They entertain and educate; they encourage imagination, curiosity, and a sense of adventure....and they delight.

Of course, they can also frustrate and annoy, and that’s why we’re here! We can help you find, and learn how to play, games of all kinds. We also develop new games.

We love hearing your gaming stories, your ideas for new games, and stories about games that are "old favorites." And, as we said above, we love playing games - and we look forward to hearing from you soon!
